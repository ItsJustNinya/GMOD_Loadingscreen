Community Guidelines

In Development • Our server is currently still being developed, expect restarts and bugs at any given time.

RDM & VDM

You must have a valid RP reason to kill or use a vehicle as a weapon against others.

New Life Rule (NLR)

Do not return to the scene of your death or remember past events for 5 minutes.

FearRP

You must value your life when threatened by a weapon or outnumbered.

Metagaming

Do not use OOC information (like Discord or nameplates) for in-game advantages.

Respect

Respect others. We don't moderate small quarrels, but we intervene if fun is disrupted.

Common Sense

If it seems like it would harm the community or experience, it is likely against the rules.
